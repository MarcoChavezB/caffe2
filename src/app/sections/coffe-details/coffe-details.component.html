Arabica

A smooth and aromatic coffee, with sweet notes and low acidity. Perfect for those who love a balanced coffee. Grown in the mountains of Brazil, this coffee is known for its superior quality and delicate flavor.

Liberico

With a strong, woody flavor, Liberico coffee is a unique option for those looking for something different. Originating from West Africa, this coffee has a distinctive character that makes it ideal for more discerning palates.

Robusta

Higher caffeine content and a more intense flavor, ideal for those who prefer a strong coffee. Mainly grown in Vietnam, this coffee is perfect for those who need an extra energy boost.

Excelsa

With a fruity and complex flavor profile, this coffee offers a unique experience in every cup. Originating from Southeast Asia, Excelsa coffee is valued for its rarity and exotic flavor.
